## Junteng Liu

I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.hkust.edu.hk/), Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, supervised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://jxhe.github.io/). I completed my B.Eng. at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) (SJTU) in June 2024.

My research focuses on natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning (ML), with particular interests in:
- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**
